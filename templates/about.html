About This Project

Any business's success depends on its employees. Businessess that realize this are concerned about employee output and productivity. Productivity has a compounding effect at the different levels in the workplace, meaning that high productivity at a lower level of organization paves way for higher productivity at the higher levels of the organization. Hence, analysis of performance of employees in any organization is the need of the hour.

It is built with Machine Learning Approach For Employee Performance Prediction.
